Meeting - June 25, 2014 | SwampSec

  • meeting announcement

This week, SIT is taking a trip to SwampSec <http://swampsec.org/>! If you don’t know what SwampSec is, it’s a gathering of infosec professionals that takes place from 7-9pm on the fourth/last Wednesday of the month downtown at Stubbies & Steins <http://stubbiesandsteins.com/>. Hope to see you there!
